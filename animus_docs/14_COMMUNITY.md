## How to Contribute

We thrive on contributions. The Animus project welcomes:

- **Bug Reports**: Join our Discord community to report issues
- **Feature Requests**: Share your ideas on Discord
- **Pull Requests**: Open an issue first to discuss your changes
- **Documentation**: Help improve documentation and examples
- **Research**: Contribute to research areas and share findings
